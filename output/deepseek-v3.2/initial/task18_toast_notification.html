Toast Notification Demo

A customizable toast notification system that appears in the top right corner and automatically disappears after 3 seconds. Try it out!

How it works:

  • Type a custom message in the input field
  • Click any of the buttons to trigger a toast notification
  • Notifications appear in the top-right corner
  • Each toast automatically disappears after 3 seconds
  • You can manually dismiss a toast by clicking the × button
  • Multiple toasts will stack vertically
