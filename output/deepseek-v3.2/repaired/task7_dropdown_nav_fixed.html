Dropdown Controls

How it works:

Clicking "Add New Item to Dropdown" will use JavaScript's createElement method to add a new item to the Products dropdown.

Each new item will have a unique name and will be highlighted with a yellow background.

Click "Reset Dropdown Items" to restore the original dropdown items.
